SUPPORT FILIPINO TEACHERS TODAY!

Educators around the world are facing emerging challenges that are now more emphasized due to COVID-19. How can we help ensure that Filipino youth continue to thrive during these challenging times and beyond?

Adopt-A-Teacher Program

Help equip and empower Filipino teachers for the next generation of Filipino students today! You can show support by sponsoring a teacher with a class voucher.

BUY VOUCHERS DONATE
By sponsoring a teacher, you will:
  • Equip Filipino teachers to use technology and master 21st Century learning skills such as critical thinking, communication, collaboration, and creativity.
  • Empower Filipino teachers to adapt to the demands of online teaching for the next generation of Filipino students.
  • Subsidize the professional development of Filipino teachers through GLA's CatalystPLUS Education Technology Certification Program.
